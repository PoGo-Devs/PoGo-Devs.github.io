Instructions

  1. Create a Pokemon™ Trainer Club account
  2. Login on the Android/iOS app to choose your starter (you can use Bluestacks)
  3. Logout from the official app
  4. Download the ZIP package for your architecture (ARM for mobile, x86/x64 for PC)
  5. Change your storage settings from SD card to phone storage (thanks to nr23730)
  6. Enable Device Portal
  7. Navigate to APPS page on Device Portal
  8. Choose PokemonGo-UWP_version.appx under "Install App"
  9. Click on "Add Dependency" twice and add the files that are inside Dependencies folder
  10. Click on "Go" under "Deploy" and wait until "Done!" is shown
  11. Launch the app on your device
  12. Login
  13. Have fun
